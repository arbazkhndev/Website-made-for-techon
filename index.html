All Time Award Winners

1. God Of War

Step into the epic world of God of War, a game that masterfully blends heart-pounding action with an emotionally charged story set against breathtaking, high-quality visuals. Follow the journey of Kratos and his son, Atreus, as they navigate the rugged Norse wilderness, facing mythical creatures, gods, and challenges that test both their strength and their bond. God of War is celebrated for its inspiring storytelling and unforgettable moments, pulling you into a world rich with history, mystery, and myth. With dramatic landscapes—from towering mountains and ancient ruins to lush forests filled with Norse lore—this game offers a visually stunning experience that feels both magical and unbelievably real. More than just a game, God of War is a powerful exploration of family, resilience, and the journey toward redemption, making it an unforgettable adventure.

Ratings Awarded: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

2. Red Dead Redemption 2

Red Dead Redemption 2 is a breathtaking journey into the heart of America’s rugged frontier, capturing both the harsh realities and the beauty of life in the Old West. Follow Arthur Morgan, an outlaw grappling with loyalty and survival, as he and the Van der Linde gang confront shifting times, powerful forces, and their own haunting pasts. Known for its award-winning storytelling, Red Dead Redemption 2 offers an inspiring, dramatic tale of brotherhood, morality, and redemption. The game’s meticulously crafted world immerses you in unbelievable, high-quality landscapes—from sprawling plains and majestic mountains to dusty towns and dense forests—that evoke both awe and nostalgia. Every detail, from the changing weather to the bustling wildlife, brings the world to life, making this game a profound and unforgettable experience that feels as real as it is inspiring.

Ratings Awarded: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆

All Time Award Winners

3. Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V takes you on a gripping, high-stakes journey through the sprawling, sun-soaked city of Los Santos, a vibrant and gritty world inspired by the glitz and chaos of modern-day Los Angeles. Follow the intertwined stories of three complex characters—Michael, a retired bank robber; Franklin, an ambitious street hustler; and Trevor, an unpredictable criminal—as they navigate a world of crime, corruption, and personal redemption. Known for its dynamic storytelling and award-winning gameplay, Grand Theft Auto V blends drama, humor, and tension in an unforgettable narrative that challenges ideas of loyalty, ambition, and survival. The game creates a truly immersive environment, from bustling city streets and luxurious mansions to serene beaches and rugged mountains. It’s more than just a game—it’s a dramatic, thought-provoking journey that brings both the highs and lows of the American dream to life.

Ratings Awarded: ★ ★ ★ ★ ★

4. The Last of Us

The Last of Us is an emotional and unforgettable journey through a post-apocalyptic world, renowned for its powerful storytelling and deeply human themes. In a setting where civilization has collapsed, follow the harrowing story of Joel, a hardened survivor, and Ellie, a young girl who may hold the key to humanity’s future. Together, they journey across dangerous and breathtakingly crafted landscapes, from overgrown cities to abandoned towns and dense forests, each filled with high-quality, realistic detail that brings this haunting world to life. The Last of Us is more than a survival story; it’s an inspiring, heart-wrenching exploration of love, loss, and hope. With award-winning storytelling, dramatic gameplay, and characters who feel achingly real, this game offers a deeply immersive experience that stays with players long after the credits roll.

5. Dying Light

Dying Light thrusts you into a heart-pounding, immersive world where survival is an art and every choice can mean life or death. Set in the quarantined city of Harran, you play as Kyle Crane, an undercover operative navigating an environment overrun by infected creatures, hostile humans, and the unforgiving rules of a crumbling society. Known for its unique, award-winning parkour mechanics, Dying Light combines intense, fast-paced action with strategic gameplay, making every rooftop and dark alley a thrilling escape. The game’s high-quality graphics create an unforgettable atmosphere, from sunlit ruins and graffiti-strewn streets to eerie, abandoned buildings that transform into deadly traps at nightfall. Dying Light offers an inspiring and dramatic experience that captures both the terror and resilience of humanity, giving players a gripping story of survival in a beautifully detailed, unpredictable world.

Ratings Awarded: ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆
